What is Gaming Addiction?

Video game addiction (internet gaming disorder) is characterized by severely reduced control over gaming habits, resulting in negative impacts on daily functioning, including personal, social, educational, and occupational responsibilities. (Cleveland Clinic, 2024)

What Do We Do?

We provide support and resources to individuals struggling with gaming addiction. Our organization helps them regain control over their lives through counseling, education, and recovery programs.

How Can We Help?

Our programs are designed to address the emotional, psychological, and behavioral aspects of gaming addiction. We offer tailored solutions to suit each individual’s unique situation and guide them toward healthier habits.

Our Course

Our specialized course equips individuals with the skills to overcome gaming addiction through daily exercises, accountability practices, and expert advice. Participants are encouraged to make gradual lifestyle adjustments that lead to sustainable recovery.
